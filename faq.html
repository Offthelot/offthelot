FAQ + Answers

What is car detailing?

Car detailing is a comprehensive cleaning and restoration process for a vehicle, both inside and outside. It involves cleaning, polishing, and protecting the vehicle's surfaces to enhance its appearance and condition.

Do you offer any discounts or loyalty programs for repeat customers?

What should I do to prepare my vehicle for detailing?

Prior to detailing, it's recommended to remove all personal items from your vehicle, empty the trunk, and ensure the vehicle is easily accessible to the detailers. Clearing out clutter helps the detailing process go smoothly.

How long does a car detailing session take?

The duration of a car detailing session varies based on the service level and the vehicle's condition. On average, it may take several hours to a full day to complete a comprehensive detailing.

Do you offer mobile detailing services?

Yes, we offer mobile detailing services for your convenience. Our team can come to your location to perform the detailing, ensuring a hassle-free experience.

How often should I get my car detailed?

The frequency of car detailing depends on factors like your driving habits and environmental conditions. As a general guideline, we recommend maintence details every 1 to 3 months to maintain its appearance and condition with comprehensive details every 6 months to a year for maximal longevity.

What is the difference between car detailing and car washing?

Car detailing is a more comprehensive process than car washing. While car washing primarily involves cleaning the exterior, car detailing includes cleaning, polishing, and protecting both the interior and exterior surfaces of the vehicle.

Do I need to remove personal items from my car before detailing?

Yes, it's advisable to remove personal items from your car before detailing. This ensures that our detailers can access all areas of the vehicle and perform a thorough job without any obstructions.

Can detailing improve the resale value of my car?

Yes, detailing can enhance the resale value of your car. A well-maintained and detailed vehicle is more attractive to potential buyers and can fetch a higher price in the resale market.

Is detailing suitable for older or high-mileage vehicles?

Yes, detailing is suitable for older and high-mileage vehicles. It can help rejuvenate the appearance and condition of these vehicles, making them look and feel better, regardless of their age.

Do you offer paint protection or ceramic coating services?

No, we do not offer paint protection or ceramic coating services as our operations are mobile. These types of services require a controlled environment to ensure proper application and curing of the product, which cannot be guaranteed in a mobile setting. We focus on providing high-quality services that are suited to a mobile operation, ensuring convenience without compromising on the finish.

What's the recommended maintenance after car detailing?

After car detailing, it's essential to maintain the vehicle's cleanliness. Regular washing and occasional touch-ups will help preserve the detailing work and keep your car looking its best. Our Loyalty program is the perfect solution for all your maintence needs.

Can detailing remove tar, tree sap, or road paint from my car's exterior?

Yes, detailing can remove substances like tar, tree sap, and road paint from your car's exterior. Our detailers use specialized products and techniques to safely eliminate such contaminants and restore your car's finish.
